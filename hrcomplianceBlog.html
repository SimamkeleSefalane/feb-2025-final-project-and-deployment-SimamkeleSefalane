HR Compliance Platform

The HR Compliance Platform was built to help businesses track and manage HR policies with ease. Using Microsoft Power Apps, I created a low-code solution that enabled HR departments to digitize compliance workflows.

Highlights & Challenges

  • Automated reminders for policy updates and acknowledgment tracking.
  • Dashboards for HR managers to view employee compliance status in real-time.
  • Ensuring role-based access and data privacy was a major challenge within Power Apps' structure.

What I Learned

This project sharpened my ability to create scalable, low-code enterprise apps. It taught me how to navigate the Power Platform ecosystem and translate business needs into fully functional solutions.
