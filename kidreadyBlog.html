Building the Kid Ready App

The Kid Ready app was born out of a passion for supporting early childhood mental health. This project aimed to create a safe, engaging space where young children and parents can access mental wellness resources designed specifically for early development stages.

Highlights & Challenges

  • Designed with empathy and research into early childhood psychology.
  • Developed a playful, intuitive interface suitable for toddlers and parents alike.
  • Balancing a child-friendly UI with meaningful educational content was the most complex challenge.

What I Learned

This project taught me how to design with empathy, especially for vulnerable user groups like children. I gained valuable experience building UX flows that cater to both adults and children simultaneously.

It affirmed my passion for creating tech that promotes wellness and inclusivity. Want to see more?
