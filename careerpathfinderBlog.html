Career Path Finder

Career Path Finder was designed to help students make informed subject choices that align with their future goals. It’s an educational tool that connects subject selections in high school to potential career paths.

Highlights & Challenges

  • Interactive design showing which careers are linked to chosen subjects.
  • Mobile-friendly UI for use in classrooms or at home.
  • The main challenge was presenting complex educational data in a simplified and engaging format.

What I Learned

This project helped me think from the perspective of both students and educators. I practiced turning research into UI logic and honed my skills in front-end development with a focus on accessibility and clarity.
